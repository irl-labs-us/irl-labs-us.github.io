The App

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

A few tabs. Infinite real-life moments. IRL keeps your social life organized without the noise.

Scenes

Create hangouts — live right now, upcoming events, or recurring plans. See friends' Scenes and RSVP in one tap.

Circles

Group your connections into Circles — work friends, close friends, neighbors — and share Scenes with exactly the right people.

Profile

Set your interests and what you're "Down For" — meeting new people, dating, deepening friendships, or hosting events.

How It Works

Built for the way
real friendships work.

🎬

Create a Scene

Post what you're up to — right now, this weekend, or recurring. Add a place, time, and vibe. Your Scene goes live instantly.

🔒

Share with Circles

Control exactly who sees your plans. Share with Work friends, Close friends, or everyone. No public blasts.

✅

RSVP in One Tap

Going or can't go — that's it. See who's confirmed in real time. No group chats needed.

📍

Real Places

Attach real venues to your Scenes. Tap a location to see what's nearby, what's happening there, and what your connections recommend.

⚡

Live Scenes

Mark a Scene as Live and friends can see you're out right now with time remaining. Real spontaneity, finally.

🤝

Add via QR

Add new connections in person with your QR code. No phone number required. Just scan and you're connected.
