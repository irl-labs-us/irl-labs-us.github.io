IRL

A safety-first social layer for real life

Built for connection, not content

Find your people — in real life.

IRL helps people create and foster meaningful offline connections through empathy, trust and safety, and shared interests and values. It’s designed to get you off your phone and into real-world moments that actually matter.

Mission

Help people feel less alone by making it easier and safer to connect in person.

Principle

Human-scale networks, interest-based circles, and intentional presence.

Design

Low-noise, low-pressure UI that nudges connection without forcing performance.

The IRL model

Social Circles organize people by shared interests and context — not followers or popularity.

Scenes describe anything from planned events to spontaneous “I’m here, join me” moments.

Personas adapt suggestions based on who you are in that moment, without turning life into content.

Safety-first defaults ensure you always control reach, visibility, and presence.

How IRL works

A simple flow designed to support real-world connection without overwhelming users.

1. Set intent

Tell IRL what you’re here for

Deepen friendships, meet new people, discover places, date, or explore — your intent shapes everything.

2. Build circles

Interest-based connections

Organize people into circles like art friends, neighbors, or nightlife — keeping networks human-sized and relevant.

3. Discover scenes

Map-first exploration

Find places and scenes that match your vibe, your circles, and your current persona.

4. Connect safely

Scoped presence

Share where you are or what you’re open to — only with the people and circles you choose.
